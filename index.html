Who are we?

"Tech Inside" is an ITES/BPO company in Bangladesh that was founded in February 2022 and offers all forms of outsourced services with a skilled workforce. With a mission to transform how individuals conduct business and interact with their customers or consumers, Tech Inside was formed. ITES/BPO Company with the longest period of experience on providing Contact Center & Back Office Support service such as Virtual Assistant, Inbound/Outbound Call center, Telemarketing, Lead Generation, Data Entry and Others. We do things differently, passionately and with fervor. We think that "outsourcing" is not the only thing that the BPO sector does. By attracting new clients, keeping hold of current ones, and delivering top-notch customer service, it symbolizes a continuous expansion process, a strategy that has proven to be profitable. We provide a wide range of call center services, including prospecting for new clients, effectively managing data, and troubleshooting administration and technical issues. Our wide range of ancillary activities are intended to advance your company through a combination of labor, technology, analysis, and research.
